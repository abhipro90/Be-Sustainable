About Us

We (Be Sustainable Staff) are a group of students who are the IB
(International Buccularate) school AICS. In IB, we need to do
SASA or Service in Action and for our SASA we chose to analyse
food and plastic waste, in our own school. We then made photos of
food and plastic waste in our school(See What we do) made
infographics representing this data, this website one of them.

We hope by making this website other student in IB schools take
inspiration from our idea.
