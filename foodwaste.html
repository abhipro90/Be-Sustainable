Food Waste

Looking at just The Netherlands, billions worth of food is scorched away every year. While intermittently, around the world so many are dying of hunger. How can people feast and waste on food while people in poverty are starving? Because people who are not in financial troubles do it without even thinking about it. Consumers are the main source of food wastage. Excessive food is bought at the supermarket and then forgotten about once it is brought home. Then 1 by 1 food starts being wasted due to expiry and bringing too much to your plate. Each time you give yourself the satisfaction that it is not ‘that much’ but it piles up. Now that we see that so much food is wasted in the bins in our school and we found from research how bad it is to waste food, we are working to stop food waste.
